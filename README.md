## About Me

With a strong foundation in Computer Science and Mathematical Finance from NYU's Courant Institute and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from National Taiwan University, I have dedicated my career to developing innovative technology solutions for the financial industry through working at hedge funds such as Paloma Partners and Citadel, then Polypaths, founded by legendary Stanley Diller and Rick Huang.

Over two decades of professional experience have enabled me to lead major initiatives, including designing enterprise-level fixed income analytic software, managing robust high throughput distributed processing system, and architecting proprietary systems for securities lending and trading. My journey has allowed me to contribute to transformative projects that emphasize operational excellence and technological innovation.
